Job Specifications and Required Qualifications:

Position Title: Junior Web Developer

Job Description:

As a Junior Web Developer at Hot Beans Web Development Company, you will play a vital role in assisting with the design, development, and maintenance of web-based applications and digital solutions. Working closely with senior developers and project managers, you will contribute to the creation of user-friendly interfaces and seamless online experiences for our clients.

Key Responsibilities:

  1. Collaborate with the development team to translate design wireframes into functional web pages using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
  2. Assist in the implementation of responsive design principles to ensure optimal viewing experiences across various devices and screen sizes.
  3. Participate in code reviews and contribute to the continuous improvement of coding standards and best practices.
  4. Troubleshoot and debug issues with web applications to ensure smooth performance and functionality.
  5. Stay updated with the latest web development trends and technologies, proactively seeking opportunities for skill enhancement and growth.

Required Qualifications:

  1. Proficiency in front-end web development languages and frameworks, including HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript.
  2. Strong understanding of responsive web design principles and experience with front-end frameworks such as Bootstrap or React.js.
  3. Basic knowledge of back-end development concepts and technologies, such as Node.js and Express, is a plus.
  4. Excellent problem-solving skills and the ability to work effectively in a collaborative team environment.
  5. Strong attention to detail and a passion for creating user-friendly and visually appealing web interfaces.
  6. Proactive learner with a willingness to adapt to new technologies and contribute to the success of the team.

Preferred Qualifications:

  1. Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related field, or equivalent work experience.
  2. Previous internship or work experience in web development or related fields.
  3. Familiarity with version control systems such as Git and collaborative development workflows.
  4. Experience with graphic design software such as Adobe Photoshop or Sketch.
  5. Knowledge of SEO principles and best practices for optimizing web content and performance.
  6. Excellent communication skills and the ability to effectively communicate technical concepts to non-technical stakeholders.

Note: While the above qualifications are preferred, we welcome applications from individuals who demonstrate a strong passion for web development and a willingness to learn and grow in a dynamic environment.
